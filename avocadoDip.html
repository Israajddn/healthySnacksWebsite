Avocado Dip

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Ripe Avocado
  • 3 tbsp Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 Garlic Clove, minced
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Spoon the flesh of the avocado into a food processor along with the other ingredients.
  2. Process until smooth, scraping down the sides as necessary.
  3. Taste and season
